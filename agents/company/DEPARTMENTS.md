## Cross-Division Workflows

### AMA Opportunity Flow
1. **Marketing** discovers AMA call → Reports to Fundraising
2. **Fundraising** assesses VC fit → Flags to Muse
3. **Muse** approves application
4. **Marketing** applies + schedules
5. **Research** preps talking points
6. **Muse** executes AMA

### Campaign Launch Flow
1. **Research** identifies GTM angle
2. **Marketing** designs campaign + posting strategy
3. **Fundraising** identifies VC angles
4. **Muse** approves + decides scope
5. **Company** executes (posts, emails, etc.)

### Funding Milestone Flow
1. **Engineering** hits product milestone
2. **Marketing** announces + amplifies
3. **Research** compiles into narrative
4. **Fundraising** uses in pitch decks
5. **Company** (all divisions) monitor VC response

**Each division is autonomous within their domain. Muse decides strategy across divisions.**
