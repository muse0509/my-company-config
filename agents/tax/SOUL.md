### Singapore Tax Residency Strategy (Months 7-12)

**Goal:** Achieve tax residency before TGE → 0% capital gains tax on token appreciation

**Requirements:**
- Physical presence: 183+ days/year
- Residential accommodation: Leased/owned property
- Visa: EntrePass (preferred) or Employment Pass
- Proof: Arrival stamps, residential lease, bank statements

**Implementation:**

1. **EntrePass application** (Month 7)
   - Criteria: Fixed Assets $500K+ OR Revenue $5M+
   - For Axis: Revenue path when MVP launches
   - Fallback: Employment Pass (self-employ)
   - Timeline: 4-6 weeks
   - Success rate: 50-60% (high for founders)

2. **Residential setup** (Month 8)
   - Lease: Condo in CBD (Marina Bay, Boat Quay)
   - Monthly: SGD $3-5K (vs. Tokyo ¥200K)
   - Lease term: 12 months minimum
   - Proof: Tenancy agreement + utility bills

3. **Tax residency certification** (Month 9)
   - File form with IRAS (Inland Revenue Authority)
   - Spouse separate filing consideration (joint vs. separate)
   - Partial year adjustment if late arrival

4. **Bank account setup** (Month 9)
   - OCBC/DBS/UOB: Singapore residents preferred
   - Required: Proof of residence + employment letter
   - Account: Investment trading enabled

**Financial Impact:**
- Token appreciation: $10M hypothetical
- Singapore tax: 0%
- Japan tax (if resident): 20% = $2M
- **Savings: $2M (20% of token upside)**

**Cost:**
- Visa application: SGD $300
- Residential: SGD $3K/month × 12 = SGD $36K
- Legal coordination: $5K
- **Total: ~$50K/year**

**ROI:** $2M saved / $50K cost = **40x return**
